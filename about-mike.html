About Mike

Driven by purpose. Grounded in experience. Focused on impact.

My career spans over 30 years in IT — but I didn’t just work in tech. I lived it. I’ve helped small businesses stay afloat through crisis, secured networks before “cybersecurity” was a buzzword, and supported users through challenges both technical and human. I’ve worked lean, led from the front, and showed up even when the system didn’t.

I specialize in cybersecurity operations, incident response, and infrastructure hardening — all with a strong focus on protecting people, not just endpoints. My approach is simple: understand the risk, communicate with clarity, and take action with integrity.

In my current role as a Systems Engineer for a Managed Service Provider, I help clients secure complex environments while balancing performance, budgets, and real-world constraints. I’ve implemented endpoint protection strategies, improved backup resilience, supported Microsoft and cloud infrastructure, and worked directly with executives to help them understand their risks and their options.

I’m also the creator of #MindfulMikeSecurity, Cyber Breakfast for Businesses, and The Mindful CISO — content series designed to make cybersecurity more human, more accessible, and more aligned with leadership. These aren’t just posts; they’re part of my mission to help bridge the gap between technical teams and business decision-makers. Because security isn’t just a product — it’s a mindset.

As I complete my B.S. in Cybersecurity and Information Assurance and work toward certifications like the SSCP, CCSP, and CISSP, I remain focused on long-term goals: to become a Chief Information Security Officer who leads with wisdom, empathy, and execution. Not just a seat at the table — but a voice that guides it.

Whether it’s building systems, responding to threats, mentoring new talent, or helping leadership see the road ahead, I believe security is about trust — and I’ve spent a lifetime earning it.
