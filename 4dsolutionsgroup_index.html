Case Study: Accounting Firm Modernization

Migrated an accounting and tax firm from personal Microsoft accounts to a secure, cloud-managed Microsoft 365 environment. Implemented Entra ID P1 security policies, MFA, and conditional access. Deployed advanced email protection and EDR across endpoints. Configured SharePoint and OneDrive, trained staff, and delivered full documentation.

  • Reduced login risk with MFA and conditional access
  • Hardened email to cut phishing exposure
  • Standardized endpoint security and backups
  • Improved collaboration with managed cloud storage

Results

Smooth cutover with minimal disruption, clearer ownership, and measurable improvements in security posture.
